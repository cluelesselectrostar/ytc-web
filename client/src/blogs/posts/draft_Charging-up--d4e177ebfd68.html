Charging up.

Unlike many of my other stories, this one won’t be on trains; no photographs of trains, no endless discussions about railfan-related topics, and no cringe-y puns about trains that you might have missed anyways. Instead, this is a collection of random photos I’ve taken over the duration of the past year in London. You’d be pleasantly surprised how much I can write without mentioning trains at all! 

The format of this story is very similar to that of the one I have written in October, and those of you who are interested in more “touristy” shots (though to be fair they are taken at awkward locations at tourist attractions) should check out my last story here.
