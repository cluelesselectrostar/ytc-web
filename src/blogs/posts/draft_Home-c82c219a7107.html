When people think of Hong Kong, they think of its glamorous tall buildings and its charming city skyline. 

Indeed things can get quite a bit charismatic at twilight.

In someways, I really dislike the way I edit my photos, as it appears to convey a somewhat vintage vibe, which isn’t the vibe a modern skyline should convey. Nonetheless, this soft-textured representation really does its job in emphasising the final hint of sunlight during twilight.

Quite often when I wander through the streets of Hong Kong, I gaze into the sky, feeling lost in this forests of soaring skyscrapers. Amidst the crowds, I continue to venture through the alleyways of this city.
